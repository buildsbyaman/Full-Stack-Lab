About the Role

We are looking for a visionary Senior Product Designer to lead our core product team. You will be responsible for the end-to-end design process, from research and ideation to high-fidelity prototyping and visual polish.

At Acme Corp, we believe design is not just about how things look, but how they work. You will collaborate closely with product managers, engineers, and researchers to define the future of our SaaS platform used by millions worldwide.

Responsibilities

  • check_circle Lead design projects across the entire product lifecycle and multiple product launches.
  • check_circle Partner with engineering to ensure high-quality implementation and user experience.
  • check_circle Create and maintain our design system, ensuring consistency across all touchpoints.
  • check_circle Mentor junior designers and contribute to our internal design culture.

Requirements

  • 5+ years of experience in product design for web and mobile applications.
  • Deep proficiency in Figma, prototyping tools, and Adobe Creative Suite.
  • Strong portfolio showcasing problem-solving skills and high visual fidelity.
  • Excellent communication skills to articulate design decisions to stakeholders.
